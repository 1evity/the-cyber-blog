About Nature

20th of October
Author Information
  • Name: Mohammed
  • Socials:

In the quiet of the forest, Where the trees stand tall and proud, I find solace in the symphony Of nature's gentle, soothing sound.

The gentle breeze caresses my face, Whispering secrets of the earth, As the rustling leaves dance above, Celebrating their timeless worth.

Streams of crystal-clear water flow, Babbling their mesmerizing tune, Inviting me to pause and listen, To the wonders of this verdant tune.

Vibrant flowers, in hues so bright, Adorn the landscape with their grace, Painting a masterpiece of beauty, That fills my heart with joyful embrace.

This natural world, a sacred place, Where time stands still and worries cease, Reminds me of the simple joys, That bring a sense of profound peace.

In the embrace of Mother Nature, I find solace, strength, and renewed life, Grateful for the endless splendor, That soothes my soul and calms my strife.
